H & H Marine is a Maine corporation located in the coastal town of Steuben Maine. Started in 1984, we are the manufacturer and custom builder of Osmond Beal designed hulls.

With twelve different models now in production, ranging from 25 to 47 and with beams up to 19'2" , H & H Marine has become the leading builder of commercial fishing boats in the Northeast region for the past 12 years.*

We also do repairs and repowers in addition to actually building the boats.

Located in the heart of Downeast Maine, this area has a rich history in boat building. From sail to the early gas powered wooden lobster boats made in the last century, the transition has been made to today's fiber glass hulls using the latest materials and technology for boat construction.

Not only are our boats traditional above the waterline, but below the waterline as well. Our classic skeg-style hull offers the traditional efficiency and performance found in a true Downeast Osmond Beal design. A full keel with an integral skeg to protect the propeller and rudder, combined with the easy motion of a semi-displacement, semi-hard chine hull style make for an extremely stable, comfortable working platform.

Stable, seaworthy, efficiency, easy to maneuver: all reasons why H & H Marine has become a leader in the industry. Building quality with Downeast Pride!
